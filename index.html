About - Animal boarding with a difference

Hi I’m Adam, and I’m the owner of Small Animal Boarding Sheffield. We are a family run business and have been looking after your pets in our home for almost 5 years now. Our aim is to create an environment where your small animals feel at home and are as comfortable as possible while you’re away.

Family Environment

As we keep all pets inside in our family home, we have a limited capacity meaning we really get to know all our guests, providing a home away from home environment.

Large Outdoor Run

With over a 100 feet of green grass and 2 large outdoor runs, our garden is the perfect space for your small pets. We also offer indoor exercise spaces for house rabbits and smaller pets.

Regular photo updates

You will rest easy with regular photo and video updates of your little pets on their holiday with us, while you enjoy your own! We can send updates using WhatsApp or direct to your email.
